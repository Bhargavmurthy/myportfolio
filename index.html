how it works

Simple process.
No surprises.

I keep things simple because I find complicated processes usually just get in the way of actually building something good.

01

Tell me what's bothering you

Send me a message explaining the problem — doesn't need to be polished or technical. Just tell me what's going wrong or what you wish was easier. I'll ask questions until I understand it properly.

02

I'll tell you what I can build

I'll come back with a clear explanation of what I'd build, how long it would take, and what it would cost. No fluff, no upselling. If I don't think I'm the right person for it, I'll tell you that too.

03

I build it and keep you in the loop

Most projects take 2-4 weeks. I'll share progress along the way so there are no surprises. You can give feedback at any point and I'll adjust.

04

You get everything, it's yours

When it's done, the code, the data, and the documentation all belong to you. I don't believe in lock-in. You should be able to maintain it yourself or hand it to anyone else.
